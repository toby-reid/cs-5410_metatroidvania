# Enemies

## The Turnip

Does nothing but block the player. The Turnip is a stubborn, immovable vegetable that seems to exist purely out of spite towards the player.

Idle: Does absolutely nothing.
Attack: None.
Combat: Can be defeated only after the player has gained the basic melee attack.

## The Spiky Snail

A slimy creature that is as slow as it is determined to kill the player. Its pace is almost insulting, as if it dares the player to underestimate it.

Idle: Wanders left and right very slowly.
Attack: Damages the player when it comes into contact with the player.
Combat: Can be defeated with the basic melee attack.

## The Bat

A flying enemy that moves erratically. It's strange movement makes it difficult to defeat.

Idle: Moves randomly within a predefined area until it targets the player.
Attack: After detecting the player, the Bat flys quickly towards the player, hurtling into a diving attack when it's close.
Combat: Can be defeated with the basic melee attack.

## The Rat

An erratic opportunist with a lust for loose change. The Rat can easily be mistaken for a harmless part of the environment until it senses wealth. The rat doesn't want to fight you. It just wants what you have.

Idle: Wanders left and right.
Attack: If the player has non-zero coins, the rat jumps at the player and steals a coin.
Combat: Can be defeated with the basic melee attack. If killed while scurrying away, the rat drops the stolen coin.

## The Null Pointer

A hole in the fabric of the game itself where something should exist, but doesn't.

Idle: Does not move.
Attack: When near the player, it moves slowly towards the player. When it comes into contact with the player, the game crashes.
Combat: Can only be avoided. Attacking the Null Pointer causes the game to crash.

## The Pause Gnome

A mischevious troublemaker whose demeanor almost invites the player to befriend it. The Pause Gnome shows its true colors when the player is most comfortable.

Idle: Wanders left and right until the player pauses the game.
Attack: When the game is paused and the Pause Gnome is near the player, it approaches the player quickly. Upon touching the player, it explodes, knocking the player backwards. The explosion does no damage to the player, but can knock the player into other enemies or environmental dangers.
Combat: Can be defeated with the basic melee attack, but only when the game is paused (the player must pause the game mid-attack and then the Pause Gnome jumps into the paused attack).
